Get Serviced works a little bit like a Taxi service as we broadcast your request over our network of providers and the first to become available will call you back or email you quotes.

The benefit of finding a service provider this way is that you will find someone local which means costs should be lower and you will also have people that are available to take on new work contact you to compete for your business.

Yes you can.Get Serviced is a Call Back and a Compare Quotes Service combined which gives you the choice of finding suitable providers the way you want.

Our first available call back system will get you 3 quotes as this is a good average to compare what you will be getting. You can get as many specific call back requests as you like by simply posting your name and number to all the individual businesses.

You can do it as many times as you like - in fact we encourage you to get as many quotes as you feel you need. It is always better to make an informed decision and be sure that the provider you have chosen is the best for the job.

Our system is instant. As soon as you hit the send button - our SmartPhone App notifies all local providers instantly so as long as they are logged into the App they will get the request as soon as you post it.
