Project Management

You are here: Home \ Solutions

"Making complex technologies simple."

Your organization may have the resources capable of executing and controlling the work of the project, however, the complexity of the undertaking is causing pause and delay due a myriad of issues.IMMENCE Project Definition Session will assure that only the appropriate planning requirements have been identified and applied to efficiently produce a successful project.

During the course of this session, our Project Managers create a baseline for the "right start" and all subsequent phases of large and small-scale business technology initiatives. We apply methods that organize projects by defining the work of the project, elaborating project scope,designing a phasing strategy, establishing work duration and milestones, identifying required resources, developing risk mitigation strategies and all other elements of planning.

The duration of a Project Definition Session varies with the size and complexity of the project. Our Project Managers will discuss and assess the duration of the session during a one-hour consultation.
