Privacy Policy

    The Homestay Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information when you use this site.We are committed to preserving the privacy of all visitors to our website.We treat the information you provide to us as confidential information.

    Personal information

    • Homestay asks for some personal information when you create a Account, including your email address and a password, which is used to protect your account from unauthorized access.
    • As a member, you can create a profile that includes personal information, such as your gender, age, occupation, hobbies, and interests, plus other content, such as photos. This information may be accessed and viewed by other members.

    Uses and Information Sharing

    • Your profile information is displayed according to the preferences you set in your account.
    • You can update your profile information at any time by clicking on the "edit" button in your profile setting.
    • You can terminate your account at any time. If you terminate your account, your profile, including any messages in your inbox, will be removed from the site and deleted from our servers. Because of the way we maintain this service such deletion may not be immediate.
