Short Biography

Home / About

I have joined IIITM as Assistant Professor in October 2006 and became Associate Professor (Direct Selection) in June 2010, having multidimensional experience in both the research and development activities, particularly in the research areas of Bulk and Nano Material Modeling and in software product development as IT applications for various services.

Earlier to IIITM, I worked as a faculty of Computer Science & Information Technology (CSIT) department of G. G. University (a central university), Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. At CSIT, I have established various laboratories and also worked in various academic cum administrative capacities viz. as Chairman of Board of studies in Information Technology, Member of Academic Council of G. G. University etc.

During my stay at G. G. University, I have completed a mega project (Rs. 100 Lakh) of Ministry of Communication and Information Technology, Govt. of India, on Content creation and IT Localization Network as Chief Investigator of the project. I have post doctoral experience of more then four years in Indian and Abroad (Sweden, Italy, Japan and USA) in various capacities.

This experience in different domains of Physics has produced number of International and National Publications, includes the regular referred papers in journals, conference, seminars.

Besides my expertise in computational physics, I have also contributed to Information Technology area since my joining as Lecturer IT in 2002 at G. G. U. Bilaspur. I have written 5 e-books in Hindi (Unicode) on different subjects of Information Technology and guided number of software development projects. This has resulted into three significant awards in consecutive years; Excellence award in e-governance by M.P. Govt. in 2009, TGMC award as Best in state category in 2010 by IBM and Finalist for NASSCOM ICT Honours 2011 by NASSCOM Foundation, GOI.

Membership of Scientific Network/Communities:

I have good networking with scientists in India and abroad in the area of my research like Computer Society of India, Institute of Physics (London, CMSN Associate of Basic Energy Science, US department of Energy, International Nanoscience Community, MEGABAR (International High Pressure Community), International Association of Computer Science and Information Technology
