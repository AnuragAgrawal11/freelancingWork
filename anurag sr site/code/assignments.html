Overseas Assignments

Home / About / Overseas Assignments

After completing my Doctoral degree in Physics from Bhopal in 1998 under the supervision of Prof. R. K. Singh, I have been offered for post doctoral research from Condensed Matter Theory Group of Uppsala University, Sweden for one year in 1999 with Prof. Borje Johansson and Prof. Rajeev Ahuja. In May 2001, I have been to International Center for Theoretical Physics, Trieste, Italy as a guest researcher for a one month to understand various aspects of computational sciences. In Dec. 2001, Satellite venture Business Laboratory, Ministry of Science & Technology, Japan has offered visiting researcher (professor status) position for three years to work on the crystal growth of InGaSb through Chinese space satellite at Research Institute of Electronics, Shizuoka University, Japan in the research group of Prof. Y. Hayakawa and Prof. M. Kumagawa. From May 2006 - June 2006, Department of Physics, Michigan Technological University, USA has given me an opportunity as visiting researcher to work on DNA sequencing via nanopore with the Group of Prof. Ravi Pandey.

Membership of Scientific Network/Communities:

I have good networking with scientists in India and abroad in the area of my research like Computer Society of India, Institute of Physics (London, CMSN Associate of Basic Energy Science, US department of Energy, International Nanoscience Community, MEGABAR (International High Pressure Community), International Association of Computer Science and Information Technology
