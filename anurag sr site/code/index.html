About Me

Dr. Anurag Srivastava is an Associate Professor in the IT Department of the ABV-Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management Gwalior.
I have joined IIITM as Assistant Professor in October 2006 and became Associate Professor (Direct Selection) in June 2010, having multidimensional experience in both the research and development activities, particularly in the research areas of Bulk and Nano Material Modeling and in software product development as IT applications for various services.
